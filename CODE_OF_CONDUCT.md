# Code of Conduct

## Purpose
We want collaboration here to be professional, constructive, and focused on building good software.

## Scope
This applies to all project spaces (issues, pull requests, code review, discussions, and official project channels).

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and professional.
- Keep feedback technical, specific, and actionable.
- Assume good intent; ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Disagree on ideas without attacking people.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, bullying, threats, or intimidation.
- Discriminatory, hateful, or demeaning language or behavior.
- Sexual harassment or unwelcome sexualized attention.
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent.
- Trolling, bad-faith disruption, or repeated hostile conduct.

## Reporting
Report concerns to: **conduct@agentincommand.ai**
Include relevant links, screenshots, and context when possible.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may take action to protect the project and its contributors, including warnings, content removal, locking threads, or restricting participation.
